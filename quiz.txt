### **二、选词填空题**  
请从以下13个单词中选择7个填入空白处，每个词仅用一次。

**单词列表**：  
animated, scandalous, sniff, bemoan, informant, speculate, reciprocate, disturbingly, effeminate, decidedly, etiquette, expletive, masculine  

**句子**：  
1. She always speaks in such an ______ voice that everyone finds her stories exciting.  
2. The news was so ______ that it shocked the whole community.  
3. He tends to ______ the lack of detail in men's gossip.  
4. As an ______, she provided valuable information to the researchers.  
5. It is polite to ______ when someone shows kindness to you.  
6. The number of errors in the report was ______ high.  
7. Using strong ______ is considered rude in formal conversation.

✅ **答案**：  
1. animated  
2. scandalous  
3. bemoan  
4. informant  
5. reciprocate  
6. disturbingly  
7. expletives

### **三、英汉互译题（全新句子）**

#### **英译汉**（将下列英文句子翻译成中文）：
1. The **animated** discussion finally led to a **decidedly** clear action plan.
   ✅ **参考译文**：这场**热烈的**讨论最终形成了一个**相当**明确的行动计划。

2. He muttered an angry **expletive** when he saw the **disturbingly** low score on his test.
   ✅ **参考译文**：看到试卷上**低得令人不安的**分数时，他低声骂了句脏话。

#### **汉译英**（将下列中文句子翻译成英文，必须使用括号中提供的单词）：
1. 投资者正在**推测**（**speculate**）市场对这条**耸人听闻的**（**scandalous**）新闻会作何反应。
   ✅ **参考译文**：Investors are **speculating** on how the market will react to this **scandalous** news.

2. 在正式的商业**礼仪**（**etiquette**）中，表现出**男子气概的**（**masculine**）自信通常受到赞赏，但过于**女性化的**（**effeminate**）举止可能会被误解。
   ✅ **参考译文**：In formal business **etiquette**, displaying **masculine** confidence is often appreciated, but overly **effeminate** mannerisms might be misunderstood.
